The Problem

LATAM Is Underserved
by Traditional Finance

High costs, slow settlement, and regulatory opacity leave millions without reliable payment rails.

💸

Remittance Fees Are Too High

Traditional providers charge 5–10% on cross-border transfers. For Mexico's $63B remittance market, this means $3–6B drained annually from low-income families.

🕐

Settlement Is Slow

Bank wires take 1–5 business days. Cross-border flows rely on correspondent banking — creating friction for businesses and families alike.

⚠️

Regulatory Gray Zones

Most crypto exchanges in Mexico lack CNBV registration or Ley Fintech compliance. This exposes users to legal risk and prevents institutional adoption.

📵

Financial Exclusion Persists

46% of Mexican adults remain unbanked or underbanked. Existing crypto platforms are inaccessible, English-only, or non-compliant with local KYC/AML rules.

Our Solution

PesoLatino — The Compliant
LATAM On/Off-Ramp

A regulated, user-friendly crypto-to-fiat exchange built for Mexico, expanding across Latin America.

Crypto ↔ Fiat Exchange

USDT / USDC / BTC ↔ MXN and LATAM fiat currencies via SPEI, STP, local banks & digital wallets.

Fully CNBV-Compliant

Integrated KYC/AML (Jumio/Onfido). Registered under Mexico's Ley Fintech 2018 regulation.

B2B Crypto Payments

Checkout & POS integration for SMEs with auto-conversion to MXN. Reduces FX risk for merchants.

Financial Education
